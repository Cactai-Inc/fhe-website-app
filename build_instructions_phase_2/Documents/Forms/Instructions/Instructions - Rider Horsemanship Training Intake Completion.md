FRENCH HERITAGE EQUESTRIAN

HORSEMANSHIP TRAINING CONTRACT INSTRUCTIONS

INTERNAL USE / APPLICATION BUILD REFERENCE

PURPOSE

This document explains how the Horsemanship Training Agreement is created, reviewed, completed, and stored.

The agreement establishes the relationship between French Heritage Equestrian and the participant receiving horsemanship education services.

WHEN TO GENERATE THIS CONTRACT

Generate when:

☐ Client purchases Horsemanship Training

☐ Participant begins a structured horsemanship program

☐ Participant requires a separate agreement from riding instruction

☐ Participant is preparing for future horse ownership or leasing

PRIMARY DATA SOURCES

The contract should be populated from:

Horsemanship Training Intake Form

Internal Horsemanship Training Engagement Form

Client profile record

Participant profile record

Emergency authorization record

REQUIRED CONTRACT DATA

Required fields:

☑ Client name

☑ Participant name

☑ Contact information

☑ Program type

☑ Training objectives

☑ Fees

☑ Signature information

Application values:

INTRODUCTORY_HORSEMANSHIPYOUTH_HORSEMANSHIPOWNERSHIP_PREPARATIONSTABLE_MANAGEMENTGROUND_HANDLINGCUSTOM_PROGRAM

SERVICES COVERED

Possible included activities:

Horse handling

Safety education

Grooming education

Tacking education

Untacking education

Stable practices

Horse behavior education

Ownership preparation

IMPORTANT SCOPE DISTINCTIONS

Do not automatically include:

☐ Riding lessons

☐ Jumper training

☐ Horse training

☐ Horse care services

☐ Horse leasing assistance

Those require separate engagements.
