☐ Client wants assistance purchasing a horse

☐ Client wants representation during a purchase transaction

☐ Client needs assistance coordinating purchase activities

☑ Horse information

☑ Seller information

☑ Purchase goals

☑ Requested services

☑ Transaction expectations

Buyer Information

Horse Information

Purchase Objective

Requested Services

Used for:

Buyer Representation Agreement

Purchase Assistance Agreement

Transaction Documents

☑ Buyer details

☑ Horse details

Use conditional fields.

Show horse details.

☐ Assume purchase will occur

☐ Leave compensation undefined
