Purpose: This document explains how visitor information is collected before a visitor participates in services or enters an engagement relationship.

WHEN TO PRESENT THIS FORM

Present when:

☐ A person visits the property

☐ A prospective client observes services

☐ A guest attends an event

☐ A person views a horse or facility

☐ A non-client enters the operational area

PRIMARY INFORMATION COLLECTED

The form establishes:

☑ Visitor identity

☑ Visit purpose

☑ Horse contact level

☑ Emergency contact information

☑ Release status

Required:

Visitor Name

Contact Information

Date

Release Status

General Visitor Release

☑ Visitor details

Confirm:

☐ Visitor understands property risks

☐ Horse interaction is documented

☐ Appropriate release is completed

☐ Visitor is not performing services without proper agreement
