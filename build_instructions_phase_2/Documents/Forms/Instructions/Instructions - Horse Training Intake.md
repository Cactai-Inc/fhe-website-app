HORSE TRAINING INTAKE INSTRUCTIONS

INTERNAL USE / APPLICATION BUILD REFERENCE

Purpose: This document explains how horse training information is collected, reviewed, and converted into a training engagement.

WHEN TO PRESENT THIS FORM

Present when:

☐ Owner requests professional training services

☐ Horse requires development or improvement

☐ Client wants structured training assistance

☐ Client wants competition preparation

Do not use when:

☐ Only exercise riding is requested

☐ Only grooming/care services are requested

PRIMARY INFORMATION COLLECTED

The form establishes:

☑ Horse identity

☑ Owner identity

☑ Current training level

☑ Training goals

☑ Behavioral considerations

☑ Medical considerations

REQUIRED DATABASE FIELDS

Required:

Client Information

Horse Information

Training Objective

Current Training Level

Requested Services

CONTRACT GENERATION INPUTS

Used for:

Horse Training Agreement

Potential Related Agreements:

Horse Care Agreement

Exercise Agreement

Populates:

☑ Training scope

☑ Frequency

☑ Objectives

☑ Fees

INTERNAL REVIEW ITEMS

Confirm:

☐ Training goals are realistic

☐ Horse limitations are documented

☐ Client expectations are aligned

☐ Scope is clearly defined

APPLICATION DISPLAY NOTES

Separate:

Training

Care

Exercise

These should remain independent services even if combined into one client engagement.

COMMON ERRORS TO AVOID

Do not:

☐ Guarantee competition results

☐ Guarantee behavioral changes

☐ Represent training as veterinary treatment
