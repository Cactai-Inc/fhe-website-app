Purpose: This document explains how horse exercise requests are converted into defined service engagements.

Present when:

☐ Owner requests riding exercise services

☐ Horse needs conditioning

☐ Horse needs routine schooling

☐ Training is not the primary service requested

The form establishes:

☑ Horse identity

☑ Exercise goals

☑ Approved activities

☑ Restrictions

☑ Safety considerations

Client Information

Horse Care Agreement

INTERNAL REVIEW ITEMS

Confirm:

☐ Horse activity level is appropriate

☐ Owner restrictions are clear

☐ Medical considerations are disclosed

☐ Exercise is not being represented as training unless separately contracted

APPLICATION DISPLAY NOTES

Exercise and training should have separate pathways.

Exercise:

Maintenance / conditioning

Training:

Skill development / behavior / performance

COMMON ERRORS TO AVOID

Do not:

☐ Assume exercise authorization includes jumping

☐ Exceed stated activity limitations

☐ Promise conditioning results
